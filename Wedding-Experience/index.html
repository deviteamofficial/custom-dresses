Your Wedding Dress Experience

From the first conversation to the final fitting, our atelier follows a thoughtful process—crafting a wedding dress that reflects your story, silhouette, and vision.

01

Design Consultation

A personal consultation to understand your vision, wedding setting, inspirations, and comfort preferences.

02

Design Proposal & Estimate

We present a detailed design concept, fabric direction, timeline, and cost estimate for your approval.

03

Confirmation & Deposit

Upon approval, a confirmation and initial deposit secure your design, production timeline, and atelier schedule.

04

Fabric & Detailing Selection

Final selection of fabrics, embroidery, and finishing details to complete the design direction.

05

Measurements & Patterning

Precise measurements are translated into custom patterns for balance, structure, and movement.

06

Crafting & Construction

Skilled artisans bring your dress to life through couture handwork, tailoring, and quality checks.

07

Fittings & Refinement

Multiple fittings ensure a flawless fit, refined details, and complete comfort.

08

Final Payment & Approval

Once the dress is approved, the remaining balance is settled and the final piece is prepared for presentation.

09

Final Reveal & Delivery

Your wedding dress is carefully pressed, packaged, and delivered—ready for your most unforgettable moment.

Each gown is crafted exclusively for you. Production timelines, fittings, and payment milestones are clearly communicated at every stage of the process.
